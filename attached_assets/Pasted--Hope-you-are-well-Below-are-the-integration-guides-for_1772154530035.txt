3D Secure

The 3D Secure feature will be enabled by default for production users to help protect merchants against fraudulent chargebacks. As a result, transaction requests must include the required 3D Secure fields, which are the customer’s email address and/or phone number. More information can be found here https://www.windcave.com/developer-e-commerce-api-rest#3DSecure_Fields.
